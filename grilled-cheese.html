Grilled Cheese

Classic Grilled Cheese Recipe

Please any picky eater with this classic grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of Sara Lee Artensano bread
  • 4 tbsp of butter
  • 8 deli slicrs of american cheese

Step-By-Step order

  1. Heat a large grill pan over high heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Lay half of the bread, butter side down, in the pan.
  4. Put a slice of American cheese on each piece of bread.
  5. Cover with the remaining bread slices, butter side up.
  6. Grill until golden brown on both sides or until the cheese has reached peak melty-goodness.
